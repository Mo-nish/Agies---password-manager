üçé iOS App Store Submission

Submit Agies Password Manager to the iOS App Store for iPhone and iPad users.

  1. 1

    Apple Developer Account Setup

    Ensure your Apple Developer account is active and configured for app distribution.

    • Annual fee: $99/year
    • App Store Connect access enabled
    • Development and distribution certificates
    • Provisioning profiles configured
  2. 2

    App Store Connect Configuration

    Create and configure your app in App Store Connect.

    • Create new app with bundle ID
    • Set app name: "Agies Password Manager"
    • Select category: "Productivity" or "Utilities"
    • Set content rating: 4+ (No objectionable content)
  3. 3

    App Metadata & Assets

    Prepare all required app store assets and information.

    • App icon (1024x1024 PNG)
    • Screenshots for different device sizes
    • App description and keywords
    • Privacy policy URL
    • Support URL
  4. 4

    Build & Upload

    Build the production app and upload to App Store Connect.

    • Archive app in Xcode
    • Upload to App Store Connect
    • Set build version and build number
    • Configure app capabilities and entitlements
  5. 5

    App Review Submission

    Submit your app for Apple's review process.

    • Complete app information
    • Set pricing and availability
    • Submit for review
    • Typical review time: 1-7 days

‚ö†Ô∏è Important iOS Requirements

Ensure your app complies with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, especially regarding data privacy, security, and user experience standards.

ü§ñ Google Play Store Submission

Submit Agies Password Manager to the Google Play Store for Android users worldwide.

  1. 1

    Google Play Console Setup

    Set up your Google Play Console account for app distribution.

    • One-time fee: $25
    • Developer account verification
    • App signing key configured
    • Content rating questionnaire completed
  2. 2

    App Creation & Configuration

    Create your app in Google Play Console with proper configuration.

    • Create new app with package name
    • Set app name: "Agies Password Manager"
    • Select category: "Productivity" or "Tools"
    • Set content rating: Everyone
  3. 3

    App Bundle & Assets

    Prepare and upload your Android App Bundle (AAB) and store assets.

    • Generate signed AAB file
    • App icon (512x512 PNG)
    • Feature graphic (1024x500 PNG)
    • Screenshots for different device sizes
    • App description and keywords
  4. 4

    Store Listing & Content

    Complete your app's store listing with compelling content.

    • App description (4000 characters max)
    • Short description (80 characters max)
    • Keywords and search terms
    • Privacy policy URL
    • Support contact information
  5. 5

    Release & Distribution

    Release your app to users through Google Play Store.

    • Set release track (production)
    • Configure country availability
    • Set pricing and distribution
    • Publish to production

‚ÑπÔ∏è Google Play Benefits

Google Play Store offers faster review times (typically 1-3 days) and more flexible update policies compared to iOS App Store.
