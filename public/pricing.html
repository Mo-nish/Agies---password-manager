Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate billing accordingly.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for both Premium and Business plans. No credit card required to start your trial.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your data anytime. After cancellation, we keep your data for 30 days to allow for reactivation, then it's permanently deleted.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes, we offer 50% discounts for qualified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information.
